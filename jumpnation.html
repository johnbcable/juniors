It's beginning to look a lot like Christmas! And to celebrate the end of term the coaching team have organised their annual end of term activity. This year it will be held at Jump Nation – an indoor trampoline park based in Walsall just over 30 mins drive away from the sports club on Saturday December 10th 2pm till 4pm.

The children will enjoy a 2–hour jump session supervised by the team of coaches and assistants from Hampton tennis club who will organise lots of activities such as slam dunk competitions and dodge ball matches. There is an area to sit out and have a break if needed along with a café to grab a glass of water if thirsty.

The cost for each child will be £16.50 which will include getting special trampoline socks to wear around the park. Spaces are limited to 50 children and after the last 2 events run we predict these spaces will fill up fast so to ensure you child gets a space please return the reply slip in a sealed envelope with the full amount of £16.50 to Sam Hiskett or behind the sports club bar. This can be paid by cash or cheque (cheques made payable to Samuel Hiskett)

For any questions please speak to Sam Hiskett

Mobile – 07496757078

Email – sam.hiskett97@gmail.com

Please download, print and complete a booking form if you would like to take part.
