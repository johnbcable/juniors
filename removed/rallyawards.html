Mini-Tennis Rally Awards at Hampton in Arden Tennis Club

The club has been using the LTA Mini Tennis Awards as part of our junior coaching programme for some time. The Rally Awards will help you to see how your child is improving with stickers, badges, certificates and the Rally Awards Collector.

The LTA Mini Tennis Red Awards have been designed for players between the ages of 5 and 8 years old to help mini players take part in exciting exercises with regular rewards which encourage them to develop their tennis skills at a pace that suits them.

We have initially trialled the awards with our Mini Red classes.
