Parents! New over the Autumn 2016 school term is the Tri Tennis Tournament. This event is a collaboration between three local tennis clubs: Hampton in Arden Tennis Club, Solihull Arden Tennis Club and Coleshill Tennis Club. The aim is to provide more competitive opportunities for our junior members. Over the next couple of months each club will host at least two events where 4 players from each club will play against each other for points. These points will be added to the club's overall score with the winning club taking home the Tri TENNIS CUP!

Dates for each round can be found in the table below along with the category that match will be eg. red, orange, green and so on.

For any questions please speak to Hampton's captain – Sam Hiskett
