Tennis and Multi-Sport Camps

To join a current tennis and multi-sport camp, please download and print a booking form, complete it and and ensure it and your fee is returned to Ian as soon as possible in order to secure your place.

Apply Now

The coaching staff at Hampton regularly run tennis and multi-sport camps during the half-term school holidays and often at Christmas, Easter and during the school summer holidays.

Summer Half-Term

Tennis and multi-sport camps will run this half-term on :-

  • Tuesday, 26th May, 2015 and
  • Wednesday 27th May, 2015

Camps all run from 9.30am to 3.30pm and cost £18.50 (reduced to £16.50 if paid by Monday 18th May). Early drop offs from 8.30 am, £3.50 extra per day.

Tots Camps

In addition, we are offering half day camps for children under 6 years of age as follows:-

  • Tuesday 26th May, 2015 and
  • Wednesday 27th May, 2015

The tots camps run from 9.30-12.30 and cost £11 per child (increased to £13 if payment not received until the day of the camp).

For any camp, please do remember to bring a drink and a packed lunch.

For bookings or any other information please contact Ian Poole on 07968 451956 or via email to ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk.

Booking is essential to ensure there are adequate staff on hand. If you have not booked and turn up on-the-day, we may not be able to accommodate you.
